Research Areas

Our Research Programs

Exploring the frontiers of nanotechnology through interdisciplinary collaboration in semiconductors, nanomaterials, and sustainable energy.

Research Focus Areas

Our research spans multiple domains of nanotechnology, each driving innovation at the atomic and molecular scale.

Semiconductor Nanotechnology

Our semiconductor group focuses on developing next-generation nanostructured semiconductor devices, including quantum dot arrays, thin-film transistors, and advanced photovoltaic cells with enhanced efficiency through nanoscale engineering.

Quantum Dots Thin Films Photovoltaics Nanoelectronics

Nanomaterials Synthesis

We synthesize and characterize novel nanomaterials including graphene oxide membranes, carbon nanotubes, metal-organic frameworks, and nanocomposites for applications in water purification, energy storage, and biomedical devices.

Graphene Carbon Nanotubes MOFs Nanocomposites

Sustainable Energy

Our sustainable energy research leverages nanotechnology to develop high-efficiency perovskite solar cells, advanced battery materials, hydrogen fuel cells, and catalytic systems for clean energy generation and storage.

Solar Cells Batteries Fuel Cells Catalysis

Environmental Nanotechnology

We develop nano-enabled solutions for environmental remediation, including advanced water filtration membranes, air purification catalysts, and biosensors for pollution monitoring and ecosystem health assessment.

Water Purification Air Quality Biosensors Remediation
